History

INTRODUCTION.

POPULAR CHEMICALS WORKS (PVT.) LTD. started its mission in 1950, to serve humanity by providing highest quality medicines (CGMP). To accomplish this goal, implementation is ensured after having incorporated the concept of quality in manufacturing as well as related processes undertaken at PCW. The commitment of our management in setting highest quality standards has contributed significantly to the steady growth of the company. PCW now enjoys a highly reputable place amongst the leading pharmaceuticals companies and medical professionals at home and abroad.

  • We're Running Pharmaceuticals Manufacturing Since 1950.
  • 100% Well Managed Laboratories.
  • We Provide 100% Quality Products.

PCW is rightly proud of its technical collaboration with renowned research oriented multinational pharmaceuticals company ROTTA RESEARCH LABORATORIUM MILAN, ITLAY.

PCW has provided a fully modern and automatic independent production facility with latest machinery and quality control instrument to LES ( LABORATORIES SERVIER FRANCE) for manufacturing of their products in Pakistan.
